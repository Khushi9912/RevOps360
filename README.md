# RevOps360 – Salesforce-Inspired Revenue & Customer Intelligence Platform

## Project Overview

RevOps360 is a Salesforce-inspired Business Intelligence solution developed in Power BI to monitor revenue performance, customer retention, subscriptions, operational efficiency, and customer engagement through interactive dashboards and analytics.
The project combines KPI monitoring, analytical reporting, and strategic business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Challenge

Businesses often struggle to monitor revenue performance, customer churn, operational efficiency, and customer engagement within a centralized analytics environment. Data is frequently spread across multiple systems, making it difficult to track KPIs, identify risks, and generate actionable insights.
RevOps360 was developed to address these challenges through interactive dashboards, advanced analytics, and executive-level reporting.
